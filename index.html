Help Center

Feel free to ask us any questions about our products or services.

Q: How can I return or exchange items?

A: Yes, you can return or exchange items within 30 days of purchase.

Q: What are the shipping options?

A: We offer standard and express shipping options. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping delivers in 1-2 business days.

Q: Do you ship internationally?

A: Yes, we ship internationally. Shipping fees and delivery times vary depending on your location.

Q: How can I track my order?

A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or through the courier's site.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept GCash, Cash on Delivery (COD), and Bank Transfer. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.

Payment Methods

GCash

Pay using the GCash app. Simply scan the QR code or enter our GCash number.

Cash on Delivery

Pay in cash when your order is delivered. Please prepare the exact amount.

Bank Transfer

Transfer the amount to our bank account before delivery. Please keep the receipt for confirmation.
